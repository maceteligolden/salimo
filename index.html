About Us

Who We Are

WE ARE EBENESER SOLUTIONS

We are an agricultural, trading and processing company dealing in agricultural commodities such as cocoa, gum arabic, hibiscus and cashews among others. By adopting regenerative practices and partnering directly with farmers, we ensure fair prices, sustainable yields, and stronger food security. Our work simplifies supply chains, enhances traceability, and promotes local value addition. With technology-driven solutions, we continue to scale innovation that boosts efficiency and builds resilience across communities

  • Our Mission
  • Our advantages
  • Our guarantees

Our mission is to enhance the efficiency of commodity production, sourcing, and trade at every level, while upholding transparency and sustainability. We are committed to ensuring equitable remuneration for producers and first-mile stakeholders, thereby fostering a fairer and more resilient agricultural value chain.

We ensure fair trade with products fully traceable to their source. Our operations are efficient, reliable, and transparent, with strong local roots and global reach.

We guarantee top-quality products by maintaining strict standards and we are committed to regenerative practices. We also prioritize long-term partnerships built on trust, integrity, and mutual growth.

Our Portfolio

Founding Director

Ewa Adeyemo

After years working in food processing and innovation management across Europe and Africa, Ewa witnessed firsthand the challenges in sourcing commodities efficiently. It became clear early on, that issues like farmer poverty, equitable trade, and sustainable sourcing couldn’t be solved with quick fixes—they required innovative business solutions that benefit everyone along the value chain. By embracing technology, investing in education, and promoting local processing alongside exports, we are driving solutions that improve traceability, support farmers, and ensure customers have consistent access to high-quality products. It’s a challenging journey, but one rooted in practical solutions and lasting impact—an invitation for partners to join us in building a stronger, fairer food system

Our Services

What We Do

Agricultural cultivation

We pioneer efficient, sustainable farming on our own farms and with local partners. Our goal is to cultivate the commodities we use without harming the environment, thereby ensuring resilient food systems for the future

Commodity trading

Agricultural commodities are essential for many of our favorite products and brands. We ensure their sourcing is sustainable and fair, benefiting everyone along the value chain. By leveraging technology and innovation, we tackle key challenges like traceability and source mapping

Value Addition to products for local and international markets

Current supply chains don't favour all participants, leaving grassroots players with little benefit. We promote local processing and value addition to increase earnings for farmers and communities. This also encourages sustainable cultivation of key commodities.

Business Consulting

We believe lasting impact comes from collaboration and shared learning. By supporting businesses—from small farmers to large corporations—we help them grow, trade responsibly, and improve operational efficiency

Our Portfolio

Our products and projects

Cocoa

Mainly of the forastero variety, sourced from our plantation and other producers in the region.

Hibiscus

A flowering plant widely cultivated in Northern Nigeria, particularly of the hibiscus sabdariffa variety

Gum Arabic

A food additive also known as E414, with wide application in various industries. Our supply comes mainly from the north of Nigeria

Moringa

Gotten from a fast-growing drought resistant tree, moringa oleifera is a popular plant in our portfolio.

Cashews

The cashew market has grown in leaps in the past few years particularly with market trends like the switch to non-dairy products serving as a boost. Cashews are cultivated widely all over the Savannah regions of Nigeria. These can be supplied as shelled on non-shelled products

Cocoa Plantation

We took over management of a cocoa plantation in 2023 which also serves as a model farm to other producers.

Digital twin of product pathway

An ongoing project using both open source and in house development to develop a digital twin of our product pathway from farm/source to consumer.

Plantain cookies

A pilot project using flour made from plantains; a popular cocoa spacer plant, to produce cookies.
